What to keep

You're paying $2,435 for four cards that triple up on lounges and Global Entry. The efficient wallet is almost always one anchor + one premium card — about $1,190 — plus Delta only if you truly fly it.

Keep · no debate

Capital One Venture X

Downgrade paths — keep the points, drop the fee

Never cancel a card that still holds unmoved points. Product-change or transfer first.

Amex Platinum →

Product-change to Amex Green ($150) or a no-fee Amex to keep Membership Rewards alive.

Sapphire Reserve →

Downgrade to Sapphire Preferred ($95) to retain transfer partners (note Hyatt drops to 4:3 there).

Delta Platinum →

Product-change to Delta Gold ($150) or Blue ($0). SkyMiles stay in your Delta account regardless.

Venture X →

Keep it. (Only if ever needed: Venture $95 or VentureOne $0.)
